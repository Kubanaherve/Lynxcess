The Problem

Rwanda's Invisible Economy

Thousands of informal traders survive on Kigali's streets daily, facing evictions, income instability, and no path to formalization. Prior solutions — designated vendor zones, microfinance, cooperatives — failed to sustain them.

Our Solution

How we came up with Solutions

e-Murika doesn’t fight street vendors — it upgrades them. Here’s how the platform removes the real barriers.

1
Accessible Digital Marketplace
Vendors sell through a mobile platform without needing a physical shop, expensive rent, or complex setup. A phone becomes their storefront.
Solution: Zero Infrastructure Needed
2
Income Stability & Customer Flow
Built-in customer discovery, location visibility, and digital ordering create predictable daily sales instead of uncertain street traffic.
Solution: Predictable Earnings
3
Low-Cost Path to Formalization
Gradual onboarding into compliance: simplified registration guidance, digital records, and scalable growth without overwhelming upfront costs.
Solution: Soft Entry to Formal Economy
4
No Transition Income Shock
Vendors keep earning while digitizing their operations. There’s no shutdown period or risky business relocation phase.
Solution: Earn While Upgrading
5
Trust, Visibility & Growth Tools
Ratings, verified profiles, mobile payments, and sales analytics help vendors build reputation, access financing, and scale sustainably.
Solution: Digital Growth Engine
